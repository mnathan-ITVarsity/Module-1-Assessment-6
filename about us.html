Brand Profile & Background

NATHAN'S WARDROBE was first established in 2018 in Durban by fashion designer and merchandise executive, Nathan TW Mutseriwa. The business was temprarily closed in 2020 due to covid, prior to him going overseas to take up an international post. He has a total of 10 years of retail experience. Some of his previous roles include Specialist Buyer for Edgars, Head of Merchandise for Alhokair, Saudi Arabia, Senior Brand Merchandising Manager for Nike Africa & Senior Brand Merchandising Manager for Alshaya, Kuwait. The primary objective of the business is to establish SUITCORP as a brand destination for men's smart attire. The key product line is men's suits, complemented by semi-formal, as well as casual silhouettes, with an emphasis on Service, Quality and Value.
